Origins



In the world of Aurinn, the exact origin of each traveler is quite unique, and this special upbringing may afford them certain boons and blessings. A token heirloom, curried favor, or even a glint of gold. When choosing your origin, consider what sort of things you may want or need for your character, and what sort of story fits them best.



Guide:

  1. Select an Origin Type from the available options in the list below. You adopt this as your character's origin.
  2. Select your Origin Boons from the boon list. You can only select boons that are permitted by your Origin Type.
  3. You have three boons available. You can select a boon type multiple times in order to increase your tier in that boon.



Origin Types

The Prime:

They are the people. From the earth they rose, and by this earth were they shaped. They are the inhabitants of the Prime Material Plane and its many brothers and sisters. Rest assured, humble beginnings have no bearing on bright and promising futures. They are the people. They are modest. They are mighty.


The Prime encompasses the basic origins of most characters. This includes the Prime Material Plane, its siblings, and Aurinn itself. It is typical for the Prime to bear material wealth, as well as have good standing with factions of the realm. However, they are not born with mystic powers outside what is common for magic-wielders.


Available boons:

  • Can choose gold.
  • Can choose heirloom.
  • Can choose reputation.
  • Cannot choose power.




The Astral Vagrant

The astral sea is a realm of immense proportions, and those that live within are vast and varied. It’s not too hard then, to imagine that one among those countless many may find themselves wandering the ceaseless depths of the astral expanse. And so, from this endless stretch of possibility emerges the Vagrant. Whatever their reasons, whatever their story, the Vagrant is here. And they shall seize the day in a fresh new world.


An Astral Vagrant can bear any form of gift, power, or reputation, for their story and origin have no limits to impede them. But be warned, for traversal across the Astral Sea is not a perfected art. Oftentimes, undesirable somethings bleed through, following these trespassers… and bringing their history with them.


Available boons:

  • Can choose gold.
  • Can choose heirloom.
  • Can choose reputation.
  • Can choose power.
  • Feature (Astral Rival): Something from their plane or past is hunting them down.




The Novaborne

In this universe, the stars are fickle and vain, and they’ve found a particularly exciting way to compete over merit and glory. Around every century of time, these celestial bodies send forth a vessel. This offspring, born of star and flame, surges forth in a crystalline cocoon and descends to the material plane. They are the Novaborne, champions of their astral progenitor. Bound to a mortal end and radiant life, these avatars play the pawn in a game between stars. The Novaborne may be keenly aware or completely oblivious to this origin, depending on their parent. Yet one thing is certain: They are beholden to an undying thirst for adventure.


A Novaborne is a star-made adventurer. They often bear innate, shapeable abilities or powerful star-birthed artifacts as a result of their origin, but have difficulty building rapport with local factions, as they are privileged strangers in new lands.

Available boons:

  • Can choose gold.
  • Can choose heirloom.
  • Cannot choose reputation.
  • Can choose power.

Origin Boons

There are four types of Origin Boons, and each can be selected by spending an Origin Point. You have three, use them wisely. You can choose to invest in a number of separate boons, or use them all for a single one. Bear in mind as well that your selected Origin Type determines whether you may or may not select certain boons.
